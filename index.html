隐私政策

核心承诺：Luma 采用本地优先架构。您的健康节律、饮水、统计与提醒数据默认存储于本地设备，不上传至我们自建服务器。

1. 我们处理的数据

  • 节律数据：工作/休息时长与提醒触发记录
  • 饮水记录：您手动记录的饮水量与时间
  • 统计数据：连续打卡、趋势、里程碑结果
  • 蓝牙信号（可选）：用于离开/返回状态识别
  • 摄像头检测（可选）：仅本地实时判断在场状态，不保存影像

2. 存储与保留

  • 默认路径：~/Library/Application Support/Luma/
  • 数据受 macOS 沙盒与本地访问权限保护
  • 可在应用内清理数据，或手动删除本地目录

3. 数据用途

  • 提供健康节律提醒与饮水提醒
  • 提供趋势、统计、成就展示
  • 在会议/全屏/媒体播放时执行智能暂停

4. 自动续订订阅信息披露

  • 订阅名称：Luma Premium（自动续订）
  • 订阅周期：1个月（自动续订）
  • 订阅价格：以 App 内购买页显示价格为准（因地区可能不同）
  • 扣费账户：Apple ID 账户
  • 续费规则：当前周期结束前 24 小时内自动扣费续订
  • 取消方式：在 Apple 订阅管理中取消，当前周期结束后生效

5. 对外共享与传输

  • 不向广告网络或数据经纪商出售个人数据
  • 不向自建服务器上传本地健康数据
  • 订阅与支付由 Apple App Store/StoreKit 处理

Privacy Policy

Core Commitment: Luma is local-first. Your rhythm, water, analytics, and reminder data are stored on your device by default and are not uploaded to our own servers.

1. Data We Process

  • Rhythm data: work/rest durations and reminder logs
  • Water records: manually logged intake and timestamps
  • Stats: streaks, milestones, and trend outputs
  • Bluetooth signals (optional): away/return detection
  • Camera check (optional): on-device presence detection only; no recording stored

2. Storage and Retention

  • Default path: ~/Library/Application Support/Luma/
  • Protected by macOS sandbox and local access controls
  • Data can be cleared in-app or removed locally

3. How Data Is Used

  • Provide wellness rhythm and water reminders
  • Show trends, achievements, and summaries
  • Pause reminders during meetings/fullscreen/media scenarios

4. Auto-Renewable Subscription Disclosure

  • Subscription title: Luma Premium (Auto-Renewable)
  • Length: 1 month (auto-renewing)
  • Price: shown in-app at purchase time (varies by region)
  • Payment account: Apple ID account
  • Renewal: automatically renews unless canceled at least 24 hours before period end
  • Cancellation: managed in Apple subscription settings

5. Sharing and Transfers

  • We do not sell personal data to ad networks or data brokers
  • We do not upload local wellness data to our own servers
  • Billing is handled by Apple App Store/StoreKit
