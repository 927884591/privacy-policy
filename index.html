隐私政策

核心承诺：Luma 是一款完全本地运行的健康监控应用。我们不收集、不存储、不传输您的任何个人数据到我们的服务器。您的所有数据都安全地保存在您的 Mac 设备上。

1. 数据收集

Luma 仅在您的设备本地收集以下信息，用于提供核心功能：

  • 使用时长数据：工作时长、休息时长，用于健康节律提醒
  • 喝水记录：您手动记录的饮水量和时间
  • 蓝牙设备信息（可选）：如果您启用蓝牙检测功能，应用会扫描附近的蓝牙设备信号强度，用于判断您是否离开电脑。我们不会收集设备名称或其他识别信息
  • 日历事件（可选）：如果您启用智能暂停功能，应用会读取您的日历事件标题和时间，用于在会议期间自动暂停提醒
  • 应用使用情况（可选）：如果您启用智能暂停功能，应用会检测当前运行的应用程序，用于在全屏或媒体播放时自动暂停提醒
  • 成就和统计数据：连续打卡天数、徽章、使用分析数据

2. 数据存储

所有数据均使用 Apple 的 SwiftData 框架存储在您的 Mac 本地：

  • 数据存储位置：~/Library/Application Support/Luma/
  • 数据不会上传到任何云服务器
  • 数据不会与其他应用共享
  • 您可以随时通过卸载应用删除所有数据

3. 数据使用

我们仅在您的设备本地使用收集的数据，用于：

  • 提供健康节律提醒（工作-休息周期）
  • 显示喝水提醒和进度追踪
  • 智能暂停提醒（会议、全屏、媒体播放时）
  • 生成使用统计和健康洞察
  • 显示成就和里程碑庆祝

4. 数据共享

我们不会与任何第三方共享您的数据。

  • 不会向广告商出售或共享数据
  • 不会向分析服务商发送数据
  • 不会与其他应用或服务共享数据

5. 权限说明

Luma 可能请求以下系统权限（均为可选）：

  • 蓝牙权限：用于检测您是否离开电脑，实现自动休息追踪
  • 日历权限：用于读取会议时间，在会议期间自动暂停提醒
  • 屏幕录制权限：用于检测全屏应用和媒体播放状态
  • 辅助功能权限：用于检测系统空闲时间

您可以随时在系统设置中撤销这些权限。撤销权限后，相关功能将停止工作，但不会影响应用的核心功能。

6. 订阅和支付

Luma 提供应用内订阅服务（Premium 会员）：

  • 支付处理由 Apple App Store 完成
  • 我们不会收集或存储您的支付信息
  • 订阅状态由 Apple StoreKit 管理
  • 您可以随时在 App Store 中管理或取消订阅

7. 儿童隐私

Luma 适用于所有年龄段的用户。我们不会故意收集 13 岁以下儿童的个人信息。如果您是家长或监护人，发现您的孩子向我们提供了个人信息，请联系我们，我们将删除相关信息。

8. 数据安全

我们采取以下措施保护您的数据：

  • 所有数据存储在您的设备本地，受 macOS 系统保护
  • 应用使用 Apple 的沙盒机制，与其他应用隔离
  • 不通过网络传输任何个人数据

9. 您的权利

您对自己的数据拥有完全控制权：

  • 访问权：您可以随时在应用内查看所有数据
  • 删除权：您可以在应用设置中清除所有数据，或通过卸载应用删除所有数据
  • 导出权：您可以通过访问 ~/Library/Application Support/Luma/ 目录导出数据

10. 隐私政策变更

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。重大变更时，我们会在应用内通知您。继续使用应用即表示您接受更新后的隐私政策。

Privacy Policy

Core Commitment: Luma is a completely local health monitoring app. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data to our servers. All your data is securely stored on your Mac device.

1. Data Collection

Luma only collects the following information locally on your device to provide core functionality:

  • Usage Duration Data: Work duration and rest duration for health rhythm reminders
  • Water Intake Records: Water consumption and time you manually record
  • Bluetooth Device Information (Optional): If you enable Bluetooth detection, the app scans nearby Bluetooth device signal strength to determine if you've left your computer. We do not collect device names or other identifying information
  • Calendar Events (Optional): If you enable Smart Pause, the app reads your calendar event titles and times to automatically pause reminders during meetings
  • Application Usage (Optional): If you enable Smart Pause, the app detects currently running applications to automatically pause reminders during fullscreen or media playback
  • Achievements and Statistics: Streak days, badges, and usage analysis data

2. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your Mac using Apple's SwiftData framework:

  • Storage location: ~/Library/Application Support/Luma/
  • Data is not uploaded to any cloud servers
  • Data is not shared with other applications
  • You can delete all data at any time by uninstalling the app

3. Data Usage

We only use collected data locally on your device for:

  • Providing health rhythm reminders (work-rest cycles)
  • Displaying water reminders and progress tracking
  • Smart pause reminders (during meetings, fullscreen, media playback)
  • Generating usage statistics and health insights
  • Displaying achievements and milestone celebrations

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with any third parties.

  • We do not sell or share data with advertisers
  • We do not send data to analytics services
  • We do not share data with other apps or services

5. Permissions

Luma may request the following system permissions (all optional):

  • Bluetooth Permission: To detect if you've left your computer for automatic rest tracking
  • Calendar Permission: To read meeting times and automatically pause reminders during meetings
  • Screen Recording Permission: To detect fullscreen apps and media playback status
  • Accessibility Permission: To detect system idle time

You can revoke these permissions at any time in System Settings. After revoking permissions, related features will stop working, but core functionality will not be affected.

6. Subscriptions and Payments

Luma offers in-app subscription services (Premium membership):

  • Payment processing is handled by Apple App Store
  • We do not collect or store your payment information
  • Subscription status is managed by Apple StoreKit
  • You can manage or cancel subscriptions at any time in the App Store

7. Children's Privacy

Luma is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and discover that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete the relevant information.

8. Data Security

We take the following measures to protect your data:

  • All data is stored locally on your device, protected by macOS
  • The app uses Apple's sandbox mechanism, isolated from other apps
  • No personal data is transmitted over the network

9. Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access Right: You can view all data in the app at any time
  • Deletion Right: You can clear all data in app settings or delete all data by uninstalling the app
  • Export Right: You can export data by accessing the ~/Library/Application Support/Luma/ directory

10. Privacy Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. For significant changes, we will notify you within the app. Continued use of the app indicates your acceptance of the updated privacy policy.
