隐私政策

核心承诺：Luma 是一款完全本地运行的健康监控应用。我们不收集、不存储、不传输您的任何个人数据到我们的服务器。您的所有数据都安全地保存在您的 Mac 设备上。

1. 数据收集

重要说明：Luma 不会收集或上传任何数据到服务器。以下信息仅在您的设备本地处理和存储：

  • 使用时长数据：工作时长、休息时长，用于健康节律提醒
  • 喝水记录：您手动记录的饮水量和时间
  • 蓝牙设备信息（可选）：如果您启用蓝牙检测功能，应用会扫描附近的蓝牙设备信号强度，用于判断您是否离开电脑。我们不会收集设备名称或其他识别信息，所有检测都在本地完成
  • 摄像头检测（可选）：如果您启用摄像头检测功能，应用会使用摄像头检测您是否在电脑前。我们不会录制、存储或传输任何图像或视频，仅在本地实时分析是否有人在场
  • 日历事件（可选）：如果您启用智能暂停功能，应用会读取您的日历事件标题和时间，用于在会议期间自动暂停提醒。日历数据仅在本地读取，不会上传
  • 应用使用情况（可选）：如果您启用智能暂停功能，应用会检测当前运行的应用程序，用于在全屏或媒体播放时自动暂停提醒
  • 成就和统计数据：连续打卡天数、徽章、使用分析数据

所有数据处理都在您的设备本地完成，不会发送到任何服务器。

2. 数据存储

所有数据均使用 Apple 的 SwiftData 框架存储在您的 Mac 本地：

  • 数据存储位置：~/Library/Application Support/Luma/
  • 数据不会上传到任何云服务器
  • 数据不会与其他应用共享
  • 您可以随时通过卸载应用删除所有数据

3. 数据使用

我们仅在您的设备本地使用收集的数据，用于：

  • 提供健康节律提醒（工作-休息周期）
  • 显示喝水提醒和进度追踪
  • 智能暂停提醒（会议、全屏、媒体播放时）
  • 生成使用统计和健康洞察
  • 显示成就和里程碑庆祝

4. 数据共享

我们不会与任何第三方共享您的数据。

  • 不会向广告商出售或共享数据
  • 不会向分析服务商发送数据
  • 不会与其他应用或服务共享数据

5. 权限说明

Luma 可能请求以下系统权限（均为可选）：

  • 蓝牙权限：用于检测您是否离开电脑，实现自动休息追踪。仅检测信号强度，不收集设备信息
  • 摄像头权限：用于检测您是否在电脑前。不会录制、存储或传输任何图像或视频，仅实时分析是否有人在场，所有处理都在本地完成
  • 日历权限：用于读取会议时间，在会议期间自动暂停提醒。仅本地读取，不上传数据
  • 屏幕录制权限：用于检测全屏应用和媒体播放状态，实现智能暂停功能
  • 辅助功能权限：用于检测系统空闲时间，判断您是否在使用电脑

您可以随时在系统设置中撤销这些权限。撤销权限后，相关功能将停止工作，但不会影响应用的核心功能。所有权限仅用于本地功能，不会将任何数据发送到服务器。

Privacy Policy

Core Commitment: Luma is a completely local health monitoring app. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data to our servers. All your data is securely stored on your Mac device.

1. Data Collection

Important: Luma does not collect or upload any data to servers. The following information is only processed and stored locally on your device:

  • Usage Duration Data: Work duration and rest duration for health rhythm reminders
  • Water Intake Records: Water consumption and time you manually record
  • Bluetooth Device Information (Optional): If you enable Bluetooth detection, the app scans nearby Bluetooth device signal strength to determine if you've left your computer. We do not collect device names or other identifying information. All detection is done locally
  • Camera Detection (Optional): If you enable camera detection, the app uses the camera to detect if you're in front of your computer. We do not record, store, or transmit any images or videos. Only real-time local analysis of presence is performed
  • Calendar Events (Optional): If you enable Smart Pause, the app reads your calendar event titles and times to automatically pause reminders during meetings. Calendar data is only read locally and not uploaded
  • Application Usage (Optional): If you enable Smart Pause, the app detects currently running applications to automatically pause reminders during fullscreen or media playback
  • Achievements and Statistics: Streak days, badges, and usage analysis data

All data processing is done locally on your device and is not sent to any servers.

2. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your Mac using Apple's SwiftData framework:

  • Storage location: ~/Library/Application Support/Luma/
  • Data is not uploaded to any cloud servers
  • Data is not shared with other applications
  • You can delete all data at any time by uninstalling the app

3. Data Usage

We only use collected data locally on your device for:

  • Providing health rhythm reminders (work-rest cycles)
  • Displaying water reminders and progress tracking
  • Smart pause reminders (during meetings, fullscreen, media playback)
  • Generating usage statistics and health insights
  • Displaying achievements and milestone celebrations

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with any third parties.

  • We do not sell or share data with advertisers
  • We do not send data to analytics services
  • We do not share data with other apps or services

5. Permissions

Luma may request the following system permissions (all optional):

  • Bluetooth Permission: To detect if you've left your computer for automatic rest tracking. Only detects signal strength, does not collect device information
  • Camera Permission: To detect if you're in front of your computer. Does not record, store, or transmit any images or videos. Only performs real-time local analysis of presence
  • Calendar Permission: To read meeting times and automatically pause reminders during meetings. Only reads locally, does not upload data
  • Screen Recording Permission: To detect fullscreen apps and media playback status for Smart Pause functionality
  • Accessibility Permission: To detect system idle time and determine if you're using your computer

You can revoke these permissions at any time in System Settings. After revoking permissions, related features will stop working, but core functionality will not be affected. All permissions are only used for local functionality and no data is sent to servers.

6. Subscriptions and Payments

Luma offers in-app subscription services (Premium membership):

  • Payment processing is handled by Apple App Store
  • We do not collect or store your payment information
  • Subscription status is managed by Apple StoreKit
  • You can manage or cancel subscriptions at any time in the App Store

7. Children's Privacy

Luma is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and discover that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete the relevant information.

8. Data Security

We take the following measures to protect your data:

  • All data is stored locally on your device, protected by macOS
  • The app uses Apple's sandbox mechanism, isolated from other apps
  • No personal data is transmitted over the network

9. Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access Right: You can view all data in the app at any time
  • Deletion Right: You can clear all data in app settings or delete all data by uninstalling the app
  • Export Right: You can export data by accessing the ~/Library/Application Support/Luma/ directory

10. Privacy Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. For significant changes, we will notify you within the app. Continued use of the app indicates your acceptance of the updated privacy policy.

Technical Support

Welcome to Luma! We're committed to providing you with the best health monitoring experience. If you encounter any issues, please refer to the FAQ below or contact us.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I get started with Luma?

After launching Luma for the first time, you'll see the onboarding guide:

  1. Set your work-rest cycle (recommended: 25 minutes work, 5 minutes rest)
  2. Configure water reminder frequency
  3. (Optional) Enable Bluetooth detection for automatic away time tracking
  4. (Optional) Grant calendar and screen recording permissions for Smart Pause

2. Why does Luma need Bluetooth permission?

Bluetooth permission is used to detect when you leave your computer:

  • The app scans signal strength of your phone or other Bluetooth devices
  • When signal weakens, it automatically determines you've left and starts counting rest time
  • This is completely optional and doesn't affect core reminder functionality
  • All detection happens locally; no data is uploaded
  • Does not collect device names, MAC addresses, or other identifying information

3. Why does Luma need Camera permission?

Camera permission is used to detect if you're in front of your computer:

  • The app uses the camera to detect presence in real-time
  • Does not record, store, or transmit any images or videos
  • Only performs real-time local analysis to determine presence
  • This is completely optional; you can use Bluetooth detection only
  • All processing is done on your device to protect your privacy

4. How does Smart Pause work?

Smart Pause automatically pauses reminders in these situations:

  • During meetings: Reads your calendar and pauses during scheduled meetings
  • Fullscreen apps: Pauses when you're using fullscreen apps (presentations, games)
  • Media playback: Pauses when you're watching videos or listening to music
  • System idle: Pauses when you haven't used your computer for a while

4. How do I adjust reminder frequency?

You can customize reminder cycles in Settings:

  1. Click the menu bar icon and select "Settings"
  2. Adjust work and rest duration in the "Health Rhythm" section
  3. Set reminder interval in the "Water Reminder" section
  4. Changes take effect immediately

5. What reminder methods does Luma use?

Luma uses non-intrusive reminder methods:

  • Edge glow: Soft colored glow appears at screen edges when it's time to rest
  • Menu bar icon: Icon color changes to show current status
  • Floating panel: Small floating panel appears for water reminders
  • No popups: Won't interrupt your workflow

6. How do I view usage statistics?

Click the menu bar icon and select the "Analysis" tab:

  • View today's work/rest duration
  • View this week's health trends
  • View streak days and achievement badges
  • Get personalized health recommendations

7. What features does Premium membership include?

Premium membership unlocks these advanced features:

  • Premium fluid backgrounds: More beautiful visual effects
  • Custom themes: More color and style options
  • Detailed statistics: Longer time range data analysis
  • Data export: Export your health data
  • Priority support: Faster technical support response

8. How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple App Store:

  1. Open "System Settings" > "Apple ID"
  2. Click "Media & Purchases"
  3. Click "Manage"
  4. Find Luma subscription and click "Cancel Subscription"

9. Where is my data stored?

All data is stored locally on your Mac:

  • Storage location: ~/Library/Application Support/Luma/
  • Not uploaded to the cloud
  • Uninstalling the app automatically deletes all data
  • You can manually backup this directory to save your data

10. How much system resources does Luma use?

Luma is carefully optimized with minimal resource usage:

  • Memory: Typically < 50 MB
  • CPU: < 1% when idle
  • Battery: Minimal impact on battery life
  • Startup: Auto-starts at login without affecting startup speed

System Requirements

  • Operating System: macOS 14.0 or later
  • Processor: Apple Silicon (M1/M2/M3) or Intel
  • Storage: Approximately 20 MB

Known Issues

  • Inaccurate Bluetooth detection: Ensure your Bluetooth device (e.g., phone) always has Bluetooth enabled
  • Calendar permission denied: Grant permission in "System Settings" > "Privacy & Security" > "Calendar"
  • Glow not showing: Check if "Reduce Motion" is enabled, which affects animation display
