隐私政策

核心承诺：Luma 主要采用本地优先架构。您的健康节律、饮水、统计与提醒相关数据默认存储在您的 Mac 设备中，我们不会将这些个人数据上传到自建服务器。

1. 我们处理的数据类型

为实现产品功能，Luma 可能在本地处理以下数据：

  • 节律数据：工作时长、休息时长、提醒触发记录
  • 饮水与健康记录：您手动记录的饮水量、时间与完成状态
  • 统计与成就数据：连续打卡、里程碑、趋势分析结果
  • 蓝牙检测数据（可选）：用于判断离开/返回状态的信号强度信息
  • 摄像头在场检测（可选）：仅本地实时分析是否在电脑前，不保存影像

2. 数据存储与保留

  • 默认本地存储路径：~/Library/Application Support/Luma/
  • 数据受 macOS 沙盒与本机访问控制保护
  • 卸载应用后，本地数据可被系统清理
  • 您可在应用内清理数据，或手动删除本地目录

3. 数据用途

  • 提供健康节律提醒、喝水提醒及进度追踪
  • 在会议、全屏或媒体播放等场景下执行智能暂停
  • 展示分析图表、趋势、成就与个性化建议
  • 改善提醒准确性与稳定性（仅在本地范围内）

4. 数据共享与对外传输

我们不会向广告网络或数据经纪商出售您的个人数据。

  • 我们不将您的本地健康数据传输到自建服务器
  • 我们不向第三方分析平台发送可识别个人数据
  • 订阅与支付由 Apple App Store/StoreKit 处理，相关交易信息由苹果依据其条款处理

5. 权限与用途说明

  • 蓝牙权限：用于离开/返回状态检测
  • 摄像头权限：用于在场检测，不录制不上传
  • 日历权限：用于会议期间自动暂停提醒
  • 屏幕录制权限：用于全屏/媒体状态识别
  • 辅助功能权限：用于系统空闲与交互状态检测

您可以随时在 macOS 系统设置中撤销权限。撤销后相应功能会受限，但不会影响基础提醒功能。

6. 订阅与支付

  • 订阅开通、扣费、续费与退款规则由 Apple 平台决定
  • 我们不直接保存您的银行卡或支付凭据
  • 您可在 App Store 账户设置中管理或取消订阅

7. 儿童隐私

我们不会故意面向儿童收集个人信息。如您认为未成年人向我们提供了不当信息，请联系下方邮箱。

Privacy Policy

Core Commitment: Luma is built with a local-first architecture. Your health rhythm, water, analytics, and reminder data are stored on your Mac by default. We do not upload this personal data to our own servers.

1. Data We Process

Luma may process the following data locally to provide features:

  • Rhythm data: work/rest durations and reminder logs
  • Water and wellness records: manual intake logs and timestamps
  • Stats and achievements: streaks, milestones, and trend outputs
  • Bluetooth signals (optional): used to detect away/return states
  • Camera presence check (optional): local real-time presence analysis only

2. Storage and Retention

  • Default local path: ~/Library/Application Support/Luma/
  • Data is protected by macOS sandbox and local access controls
  • Data may be removed when uninstalling the app
  • You can clear data in-app or manually remove local files

3. How We Use Data

  • Provide health rhythm reminders and water tracking
  • Pause reminders intelligently during meetings/fullscreen/media
  • Show analytics, trends, milestones, and suggestions
  • Improve local reminder stability and accuracy

4. Sharing and Transfers

We do not sell your personal data to ad networks or data brokers.

  • We do not send your local wellness data to our own servers
  • We do not share identifiable personal data with third-party analytics
  • Subscriptions and payments are handled by Apple App Store/StoreKit under Apple policies

5. Permissions and Purpose

  • Bluetooth: away/return detection
  • Camera: on-device presence detection only
  • Calendar: meeting-based Smart Pause
  • Screen Recording: fullscreen/media state detection
  • Accessibility: idle/input state detection

You can revoke permissions any time in macOS System Settings. Revoking permissions may limit related features but not basic reminders.

6. Subscriptions and Payments

  • Billing, renewal, and refund rules are controlled by Apple
  • We do not directly store your card or payment credentials
  • You can manage/cancel subscriptions in App Store settings

7. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a minor submitted inappropriate information, contact us.

8. Security

  • Local data is protected by macOS security controls
  • The app runs in sandboxed mode
  • No personal data network transfer by default

9. Your Rights

  • Access: view key records in the app
  • Deletion: clear data in-app or uninstall
  • Export: export files you can access locally

10. Policy Updates

We may update this policy for feature or legal changes. Continued use of Luma after updates means you accept the revised policy.
